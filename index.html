Branding is the visible expression of a business’s essence.

A brand is not just a logo, color palette, or font. Those are the tools used to communicate the deeper thing:

  • who the business is
  • what it believes
  • how it makes people feel
  • what makes it recognizable
  • why someone should remember it

Your strength is not merely making something look attractive. It is recognizing the personality already inside a business and giving that personality a visual identity.
